DevExpress NuGet Feed
---------------------

DevExpress Components for Xamarin.Forms are free-of-charge. To learn more about our free offer and reserve your free copy,
please visit the following webpage: https://www.devexpress.com/xamarin-free/

IMPORTANT: You MUST register your personal NuGet package source for the solution to build correctly: https://nuget.devexpress.com/

If you are unfamiliar with NuGet packages, please review the following Microsoft help topic (to register a NuGet source): https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/nuget/consume-packages/install-use-packages-visual-studio
